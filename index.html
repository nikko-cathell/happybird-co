Welcome!

HappyBird Co. is a nonprofit company dedicated to the care and safekeeping of birds. We are passionate about education and pride ourselves on being a safe space for anyone to adopt, surrender, and learn more about one of nature’s most fascinating species.

Two years ago, our founder, Burr D’Luvar and our co-founder Parra Kiet set our company in motion using their shared love for birds and education to bring their ideas to fruition. Now, we have amassed over 20 devoted volunteers who are the life of our company. Together we have rescued over 300 birds,adopted out over 225 of those birds, and have amassed a large audience to our social media, teaching and spreading knowledge to thousands. It is our mission and goal to grow even further spreading our reach into more communities and helping birds wherever we can.
